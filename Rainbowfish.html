THE RAINBOW GOLDFISH

Rating: 8/10

Description: The colors make it fun, but the orange ones always get left behind because they're too original. Despite what everyone says, the red ones taste the best.

When to eat: With a group of friends during lunch time. Then, you can all debate whether or not the different colors taste different while avoiding getting pooped on by a bird outside.
